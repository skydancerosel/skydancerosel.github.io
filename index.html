AI / ML Research

My current work develops a geometric framework for understanding neural network training dynamics. The central observation is that optimization trajectories of overparameterized transformers collapse onto low-dimensional execution manifolds in weight space. These manifolds are empirically invariant under gradient flow — loss-landscape curvature, measured via commutator defects, concentrates entirely in transverse directions. The onset of this orthogonal curvature reliably predicts generalization transitions, and the framework extends from single-task to multi-task settings, across training regimes, and to non-arithmetic domains.
